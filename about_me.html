Olivia Heymanns

Photographer and aspiring Interaction Designer


Introducing Olivia, a 20-year-old photographer and designer from Oslo, Norway,
now making her mark in Berlin, Germany.
Since she could first hold a camera, Olivia's lens has been drawn to human interactions and fleeting moments,
capturing stories in every frame.

With a media and communication degree and A-levels in Photography and Media Studies,
Olivia's academic journey laid the foundation for her artistic pursuits.
Following a year of photography seminars, she showcased her work at Henie Onstad Art Centre,
solidifying her presence in the art world.
Currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Interaction Design at Code University,
Olivia merges her passion for photography with innovative design principles,
exploring the intersection of technology and human connection. Eager for new adventures and challenges in photography, videography, and design,
Olivia's creativity knows no bounds.
Each click of her shutter invites you to join her on a journey
of discovery through the captivating landscapes
of human emotion and interaction.
